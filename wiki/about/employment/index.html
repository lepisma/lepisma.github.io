Notes on my employment and related experiences. Past, current and future. You might also be interested in my educational background.

I am currently spending most of my time on personal projects and planning out the next 10 years or so of my life. On the side, I am open to work and collaborate on interesting short term projects. More details on that are in this section.

1. Experience Highlights

1.1. Head of Machine Learning @ Skit.ai (2018 - 2024)

As one of the initial ML hires and Head of Machine Learning at Skit.ai, I was a core member of the founding team that scaled the company from ~15 to ~400 people and achieved a successful Series B funding round.

  • Built the entire ML function from the ground up, growing and mentoring a world-class team that published in top tier venues while running and maintaining speech-first voicebots at production scale.
  • Led the end-to-end development of goal-oriented voicebots, taking them from initial R&D to large-scale deployment with major enterprise clients in India and the USA.
  • Directed strategy and execution across ML research, MLOps, system design, product development, and organizational scaling.
  • Owned the technical vision for the company's core AI systems, ensuring they remained at the cutting edge of Conversational AI and Speech Technology and stayed a constant source of competitive advantage.

As a core part of leadership and founding team, my influence extended beyond technology to shaping the company's operational and strategic foundations. I played a key role in developing org-wide processes, evolving core values and culture, defining the employee lifecycle, and driving critical cross-vertical decisions, including the company's rebranding from Vernacular to Skit.

  2. Intern, Qualcomm Research (2015): Worked on image quality tuning systems.

2. Working with Me

I am open to working on projects that lie under my area of interests and can compensate for my time. Usually this would translate to part time consulting or advisory work. In a few cases, this could be more full time but still time bound to be contained within a few months.

I am interested in projects involving Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Speech Tech, Conversational AI, among others. Work that are more broadly technical but with important and positive social impact are also something I would be happy to get involved in. More often than not, I can help you in the following ways:

  1. Technical Strategy & AI Roadmapping
    1. Defining a product vision and technical roadmap for new AI/ML features.
    2. Evaluating build-vs-buy decisions for ML infrastructure and models.

  2. ML System Design & Architecture
    1. Designing scalable, real-world systems for Speech Tech, Conversational AI, and other ML domains.
    2. Reviewing existing architectures to identify bottlenecks and improve performance.
    3. Mentoring teams on best practices for MLOps and production-ready Machine Learning.
  3. Leadership & Team Building
    1. Providing fractional or interim leadership for your ML team.
    2. Designing hiring frameworks, writing role descriptions, and interviewing senior ML talent.
